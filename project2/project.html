Project:1-Login and Register page

  • A basic understanding of HTML forms is all that is needed for this beginner-level project.
  • In this project you can registration and login on the same form.
  • The login form is displayed when you click the login button.
  • The signup form is opened by clicking the register button.
  • First name, last name, email, password, and other information must be entered on the registration form.
  • Only the email address and password are required to be entered on the login page.
